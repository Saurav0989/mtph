Projectile Range on Level Ground

A projectile is launched from level ground at speed $v_0$ and angle $\theta$ above the
horizontal. Find its range $R$ (neglect air resistance).

Answer

$ R = \frac{v_0^2 \sin(2\theta)}{g} $

Solution

The range is the horizontal speed times the time of flight. Writing the flight time as
$2 v_0\sin\theta/g$ and using $2\sin\theta\cos\theta = \sin 2\theta$,
$$R = v_0\cos\theta \cdot \frac{2 v_0 \sin\theta}{g} = \frac{v_0^2 \sin(2\theta)}{g},$$
which is largest at $\theta = 45^\circ$.
